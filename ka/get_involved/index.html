Recycle at Home

Recycling starts at home, by considering your and your family’s habits when it comes to waste. Did you know that about half of the waste in an average household can be composted, keeping it out of landfills? And of the remaining waste, up to 60% can be recycled! This means that you could reduce by up to 80% the waste you send to landfills!

The first step to getting involved is to start recycling at home. Visit “Recycling at Home” for more information.

Organize Community Bins

A great way to get involved is to get others involved! Talk to your neighbors about recycling and encourage them to participate as well. If you can organize your neighborhood or apartment building to recycle together, we can provide you with the necessary bins and our free pickup service. Contact us today to get started!

Sponsor Us

Would you like to sponsor bins for schools? Or public bins? Get in touch with us and get involved!

Volunteer!

Coop Georgia is mainly comprised of volunteers who want to make a positive change in the environment of Georgia.

Want to get involved? We always welcome volunteers to help with Coop Georgia, whether your talents are design, education, fundraising, or sheer physical labor!

Email us at recycling@coop.ge and tell us how you'd like to get involved!
