For Home

It's easy to recycle at home! We can help!

How does it work?

  1. Sort your glass, plastic, paper/cardboard, and aluminum cans.
    (You'll be amazed how much your garbage bag shrinks)
  2. Take your recyclables to our public recycling depot.

FAQ(?)

Where can I drop off my recycling?

Right now we only have one public bin location, at the old entrance to the Hippodrome. We are working to offer more public drop off points in the future. You may also consider organizing community bins.

What are community bins?

While we cannot yet offer recycling pickup at individual homes, some neighborhoods have joined together to recycle as a group. It is possible to for you to organize bins for your neighborhood or apartment building, and sign up for our recycling service. Call us for more information about organizing community bins.

What can I recycle?

  • Glass bottles/jars
  • Plastics (plastic bottles)
  • Paper and cardboard
  • Aluminum (soda and beer cans)

What can I not recycle?

We are not yet able to recycle Tetrapacks, which have paper on the outside and plastic on the inside (such as milk cartons). We also do not yet deal with compost, batteries, and certain types of plastic, such as plastic cups. We cannot yet recycle tin cans.
